Test Details

The Diabetes Prediction System allows you to predict your risk of diabetes based on various health parameters. The tests available are:

  • Diabetic Test: Predicts diabetes based on blood parameters.
  • Retino Test: Predicts retinal issues associated with diabetes.
  • Cataract Test: Predicts the likelihood of cataracts based on eye images.

Each test requires specific inputs which can be entered through the forms provided. The system uses advanced machine learning algorithms to analyze your data and provide a prediction.
